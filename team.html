Team Members

NeonY23

NeonY23 is the Lead Developer. Mainly about the technical aspects of the project like this website and then making the different custom items and making plugins work. But also contributes to the design ambiance with all the environement ideas and a part of the game design and planning phases.

With Henderfire, he is the creator of Mosslorn.

Henderfire

Henderfire is a Developer too but also works on the design aspects of the project. In particular, he focuses on creating immersive environments and enhancing the overall visual style with adding the custom mobs you will see in your game.

With NeonY23, he is the creator of Mosslorn.
