Map

This tool allows you to view different layers of the Mosslorn map. Use the slider to switch between layers. (Also, be careful, these images are quite large, so it may take a while to load them all at the beginning.)

The Layers view shows the different layers of the map, while the Zones view displays the zones of the map.
